Cart:

addToCart(int customerID, int productID, int quantity)
	Checks if customer already has that product in their cart, if they do its incremented by the quantity 
							   	   if they don't the product and quantity is added 

getCartID(int customerID)
	Used by addToCart to check if that customer already has a cartID assigned to them, if it does it returns it
										   	   if it doesn't it checks the max of the
										           cartID's and assigns it to that customer

	Used by removeFromCart to get customer's cartID
 
removeFromCart(int customerID, int productID)
	Used from ViewCart page to remove products from a customer's cart
